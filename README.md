# Robotics Learning Hub

An interactive educational robotics platform designed to teach engineering design, robotics systems, electrical concepts, and technology careers through immersive front-end experiences.

---

## Overview

Robotics Learning Hub is a multi-page educational website focused on helping students understand modern robotics systems through interactive visuals, structured explanations, and real-world engineering concepts.

The platform combines robotics education with modern web design to create a more engaging learning experience than traditional presentations or static documents.

---

## Topics Included

### Engineering Design Process (EDP)

Learn how engineers identify problems, prototype solutions, test designs, and improve robotic systems through iteration.

### Foundations of Robotics Systems

Explore robot types, sensors, actuators, controllers, power systems, and how robotic subsystems work together.

### Electrical & Electronic Systems

Understand AC vs DC power, circuits, electrical components, motor control, and robotics schematics.

### Careers & Technology in Robotics

Discover robotics-related careers, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Robotic Process Automation (RPA).

### Project Sources

A dedicated references page containing trusted educational and industry resources used throughout the project.
